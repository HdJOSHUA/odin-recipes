Rolex

Description

Rolex is a popular street food in Uganda, consisting of a chapati filled with eggs and vegetables. The name "Rolex" is a playful combination of "rolled eggs" and "chicken" (a common ingredient in the dish).

Ingredients

  • 1 chapati
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and cook the eggs until done.
  2. Add the diced vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Place the cooked egg and vegetable mixture on the chapati.
  4. Fold the chapati over the filling and serve.
